English Translation

"150 people received check-ups at the health camp; medicines distributed free of charge."

Dehradun: Residents of Danda Lakhaund showed great enthusiasm at the free health camp organized by Sky Health Care Centre Services. 150 people were examined and given medicines at the camp.

The camp, held at the Danda Lakhaund Panchayat Bhawan on Sahastradhara Road, was inaugurated on Sunday by the chief guest, Raipur MLA Umesh Sharma Kau. He said that health is the greatest asset in life and that such camps are an excellent way to make people aware of their health.

Doctors at the camp provided services including blood pressure checks, blood sugar testing, dental check-ups, general health consultations, and other health services. In addition, a yoga instructor provided guidance on yoga and shared information on health, Ayurveda, and diet.
